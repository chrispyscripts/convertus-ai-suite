Your AI chief of staff

A second brain for every role.

For each registered position — CEO, Regional Manager, District Sales Manager and any role you add — an assistant watches the inbox and calendar, prepares a morning briefing, drafts and sends replies, and quietly learns how that person works so the right things surface at the right time. It's the layer that ties every tool in the suite to each user's actual day.

📥 Email & calendar monitoring

Connected to each registered position's mailbox and calendar — it reads what's coming in, spots what's urgent, and links messages to the right account, contract or site.

☀ Morning briefing

Every role starts the day with a tailored summary: what needs a decision, what's overdue, the day's meetings with prep, and the one or two things that actually matter.

↩ Smart auto-reply & drafting

Drafts replies in your voice, handles routine messages automatically, and routes anything sensitive to you — clearing the inbox without losing the personal touch.

📅 Meeting & day prep

Before each meeting it pulls the relevant account history, contract status, open items and recent emails into a one-glance brief — no scrambling beforehand.

🧠 A second brain that learns

It adapts to how you work — your priorities, your phrasing, your recurring tasks and past decisions — getting more useful over time so less has to be explained.

🔒 Always in your control

Permission-scoped per role, with approve-before-send on anything that leaves your name on it. Your data stays in your systems.

Connects to Microsoft 365 / Google Workspace by API. The assistant's learning is private to each user and role.

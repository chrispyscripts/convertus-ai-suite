The opportunity

A growing, multi-site business with no AI today.

Convertus designs, builds, operates and services organic waste facilities for major municipalities — and just acquired AIM Environmental. That growth multiplies the paperwork, the cross-site coordination, the bids, the compliance, and the safety exposure. The website advertises smart SCADA and VR, but no AI. This suite closes that gap, mapped to the people who run the business.

Less manual load

Bids, reports, permits and inbound triage drafted automatically — freeing senior people from repetitive work.

Fewer surprises

Predictive maintenance, safety and contract alerts surface risk before it becomes cost or incident.

More wins

Tender discovery, prospect scoring and ROI tooling build pipeline in a tender-driven market.
